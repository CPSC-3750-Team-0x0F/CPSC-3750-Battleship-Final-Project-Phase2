How to Play

1. Connect: Enter a server URL and click Connect.

2. Profile: Enter a username to create or login to your account.

3. Join: Create a new game or join an existing one from the list.

4. Setup: Place your 3 ships on the grid by clicking empty cells.

5. Battle: Take turns firing at the enemy grid. Sinking all enemy ships wins the game!
